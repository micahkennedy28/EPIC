Our Mission

What Does E.P.i.C. Mean?

E

Everything

Nothing is out of reach when you walk with God. Everything is on the table — your dreams, your future, your impact.

P

Possible

With God, all things are possible. We believe in a generation that refuses to be defined by limitations.

i

In

Our strength, our identity, and our hope are found in relationship — in Christ, in community, and in each other.

C

Christ

At the center of everything we do is Jesus Christ — our foundation, our compass, and the reason we exist.

Our Core Values

Faith-Centered

Everything we do is rooted in scripture and a living relationship with Jesus Christ. Faith is not just a topic — it is our foundation.

Community-Driven

We grow together. From small groups to large events, we create spaces where authentic relationships are formed and people belong.

Impact-Focused

We are called to be the hands and feet of Jesus in our world. From local outreach to global awareness, impact is always the goal.
